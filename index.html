Call me By Your Name

A coming-of-age novel by André Aciman about a summer romance in Italy between Elio, a 17-year-old American-Italian Jewish boy, and Oliver, a 24-year-old American Jewish scholar. The story delves into the intensity and complexities of their relationship and its profound effects on their lives​​.

Price: $15.99

It Ends With Us

Colleen Hoover's 2016 romance novel follows Lily Bloom and her tragic romance with Ryle Kincaid. It reflects on Lily's past in an abusive home, her subsequent abusive relationship, and her journey towards liberation. The book is a reflection on Hoover's own family history and has sold over a million copies worldwide​​.

Price: $15.08

They Both Die At The End

A young adult science fiction novel by Adam Silvera, where two teenage boys, Mateo and Rufus, are informed by Death-Cast that they have only twenty-four hours to live. They meet through the Last Friend app and spend their final day together, finding hope and love even as their end approaches​​.

Price: $15.99

All The Light We Cannot See

Anthony Doerr's novel set during World War II, follows a blind French girl and a German orphan boy, whose paths collide amidst the turmoil of war. It emphasizes the significance of the narratives of ordinary people during such devastating times​​.

Price: $19.51

The Alchemist

Paulo Coelho's story of Santiago, a shepherd on a quest to find a treasure in Egypt that he has seen in a dream. His journey is filled with people who guide and test him, and the novel serves as an allegory about finding one's own path in life and following one's dreams​​.

Price: $21.99

The Fault In Our Stars

John Green's novel about Hazel, a sixteen-year-old girl with thyroid cancer. She meets and falls in love with Augustus at a cancer support group. Her life takes an unexpected turn when she meets her favorite author, challenging her preconceived notions​​.

Price: $20.49

Verity

In this psychological thriller by Colleen Hoover, Lowen Ashleigh, a struggling writer, is hired to complete the bestselling series of incapacitated author Verity Crawford. Lowen uncovers a secret autobiography revealing disturbing secrets about Verity's life and family, plunging her into a deepening mystery​​.

Price: $16.25

Howl's Moving Castle

This beloved fantasy novel by Diana Wynne Jones chronicles the adventure of Sophie Hatter, cursed with an old body by the Witch of the Waste. Seeking to break the curse, she encounters Wizard Howl, his apprentice Michael, and Calcifer the Fire Demon, embarking on an enchanting quest​​.

Price: $12.99

The Love Hypothesis

Ali Hazelwood's romance novel features Olive, a Ph.D. candidate who fakes a relationship by kissing a stranger, only to discover he is the formidable Professor Adam Carlson. The book follows their comedic and tumultuous path to finding true love​​.

Price: $22.00

Five Feet Apart

Written by Rachael Lippincott with Mikki Daughtry and Tobias Iaconis, this novel explores the romance between two teenagers with cystic fibrosis. Despite the physical barriers set by their condition, they develop a relationship that questions the essence of living a full life​​.

Price: $15.99

Shatter Me

Tahereh Mafi's young adult dystopian thriller introduces Juliette, a 17-year-old girl with a deadly touch. The narrative style is distinct, featuring strikethrough text, resembling a personal diary and reflecting Juliette's tumultuous thoughts​​.

Price: $19.99

Twisted Love

In Ana Huang's romantic suspense novel, Ava Chen is guarded by her brother's friend, the enigmatic Alex Volkov, after her brother leaves for a year. Ava and Alex's ensuing relationship is filled with passion and danger, promising to alter their lives irrevocably​​.

Price: $23.76
